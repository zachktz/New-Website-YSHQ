Our story

Two lifelong friends. One vision.

Before Your Social HQ, we'd already been building audiences for other people for years. Zach was leading social at Monster Energy — working across some of the most content-forward brands on the planet. Carson had been in the space just as long, obsessed with what actually makes short-form perform.

The pattern we kept seeing: local operators with great businesses, real stories, real reputations — but totally invisible on social. Not because they didn't deserve attention. Because nobody was building for them the way the big brands get built for.

Same rigor the Fortune 500 gets — applied to the brands shaping their own communities. — The thesis

So we built it ourselves. Same creative standards, same hands-on obsession — applied to the brands that mean business in their own markets.

And yes — we've been friends for most of our lives. That's not a coincidence. You don't build something like this with just anyone.

Co-Founder

Zach Katz

Zach spent years on the social team at Monster Energy, one of the most aggressive content brands in the world, where he ran the Monster Army account and grew it from 60K to 350K followers. He worked with brands like Reign Bodyfuel on content strategy, traveled the country producing content, and sat in monthly meetings with the heads of social at Instagram and TikTok. He left Monster with a firsthand understanding of every major platform and what it actually takes to grow an account.

C
Co-Founder

Carson Fassett

Carson has been in sales and marketing since graduating high school. Over the past seven years he's generated leads for small businesses by building end-to-end funnels and running paid channels. That work helped scale Mastiff Moving in San Diego from zero to three trucks and six-figure months. One problem kept showing up everywhere: when the budget ran out, the results ran out with it. The only marketing that kept paying was a brand people actually trust, built on social. That's why he co-founded YSHQ, and that's the playbook he runs for clients today.

Plus our crew — editors, strategists, & content innovators behind the scenes on every account.
